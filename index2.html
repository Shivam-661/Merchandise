CIU Custom Lab: Personalized Apparel & Accessories | Custom Collection

Welcome to CIU Custom Lab — the personalization hub by Cover It Up that brings your creativity to life. Whether you’re designing for yourself, your business, a special occasion, or as a meaningful gift, our Custom Collection offers high-quality, fully customizable apparel and accessories. With easy-to-use tools and a wide range of products, we empower you to design your own products exactly how you want them.

From custom t-shirts and custom hoodies to personalized mugs, customized phone cases, and tote bags, we make it easier than ever to create something unique, stylish, and entirely yours.

What is CIU Custom Lab?

CIU Custom Lab is a one-stop platform created by Cover It Up to help individuals and businesses express themselves through custom merchandise. Whether you're launching a clothing line, celebrating a milestone, or simply want a cool t-shirt with your pet's face on it — we've got you covered. Our mission is simple: to make custom apparel and personalized accessories accessible, affordable, and high quality.

What Can You Customize?

  • Custom T-Shirts: Perfect for events, teams, brands, or just everyday wear. Choose from a variety of colors, sizes, and fits.
  • Custom Hoodies: Stay warm and stylish with personalized hoodies featuring your own design, artwork, or message.
  • Customized Phone Cases: Protect your device in style with phone cases designed by you—add your name, favorite quotes, or images.
  • Custom Mugs: Great for gifting or personal use, you can add photos, text, or artwork to create a truly unique mug.
  • Personalized Tote Bags: Eco-friendly and fashionable, these are perfect for shopping, gifting, or branding.

Why Choose CIU Custom Lab?

  • Premium Materials: All of our custom products are made with top-quality fabrics and printing methods for vibrant, long-lasting results.
  • Easy Design Tools: You don’t need to be a designer! Our intuitive platform lets you upload images, add text, and adjust layouts easily.
  • Wide Range of Products: Whether it's custom apparel or personalized gifts, we offer something for everyone.
  • Perfect for Any Occasion: Be it weddings, birthdays, company events, or promotional campaigns—CIU Custom Lab makes personalization simple and fun.
  • Fast, Reliable Shipping: We make sure your customized products reach you on time and in perfect condition.

How to Design Your Own Product

Creating your own personalized product with CIU Custom Lab is easy. Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Select a Product: Start by browsing our custom collection. Choose from apparel like t-shirts and hoodies, or accessories like mugs and phone cases.
  2. Customize: Use our design tool to add images, text, logos, or artwork. Play with colors, layouts, and fonts until it’s perfect.
  3. Preview & Order: Preview your final product, check details, and place your order. We’ll take care of the rest!

Who we are
With unique strategies and a notion to think outside the box, Cover It Up is a leading shopping portal in India. Tied up with all the major franchises, we look to bring the best of all worlds, with a dash of our own style. With a penchant for creativity, and a simple idea with big dreams, Cover It Up was started in 2013. Realizing the difficulty in getting our hands on premium merch in India, Cover It Up was established to change this for the better. We have worked really hard to build a platform to make sure the ever-present Pop Culture shares the same limelight in India just as much as anywhere else. More than hard work and perseverance, this can be attributed to something much more basic- love and adoring the figures we grew up with and wanting to make them a part of our everyday lives. Our biggest strength is our team. A lovely group of enigmatic people who share a collective love for Pop Culture, our team finds pleasure in devising both innovative and creative ideas to help us realize our dream of giving the Indian market merchandise from their favourite franchises, while making sure the signature of Cover It Up is not lost on them.
